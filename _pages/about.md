I am a PhD student in Political Science at [Duke University](https://polisci.duke.edu/) studying Security, Peace, and Conflict. Additionally, I am affiliated with Dr. Andrew Shaver's [Political Violence Lab](https://www.politicalviolencelab.com/) where I was a predoctoral fellow. 

I received my MS from the University of Utah in Political Science and my B.A. from the University of Arkansas in Middle East Studies/ Arabic. Currently, I am engaging in projects investigating the determinants of forced migration and the effects that refugees have on host communities. I have general substantive interests in political violence, substate conflict, and international development. Methodologically I am interested in computational social science and leveraging microlevel data to uncover causal relationships.
